Have you heard about Tetris?

Tetris is SUPER FUN

Tetris, created by Alexey Pajitnov in 1984, is a classic puzzle video game featuring falling geometric shapes called Tetriminos. Players manipulate and arrange these pieces to complete horizontal lines, earning points and preventing the screen from filling up. Its simple controls and strategic depth make it accessible to a wide audience. Tetris has become a timeless favorite across various platforms, from its original release on the Electronika 60 to global success on the Nintendo Game Boy. Its iconic music and addictive gameplay, coupled with its impact on cognitive research, have solidified Tetris as a cultural phenomenon in the world of video games.
